Vocabulary Terms

Terms

List of terms for use. Please note this list is under active editing. There are many terms very close to each other like geoscience and geoscientist. When you can use the "field of study" not the profession.

We will be both reconciling these similar terms in the list as well as providing definitions for the terms to ensure agreement on use.

You do NOT need to use these terms if you believe they do not match up with the resource you are describing.
