Paleogeosciences Web Resources Data Set

A collection of mostly web based resources associated with paleogeosciences. These include links to tools, data collections, repositories and web sites that serve the paleontology and paleogeoscience community.

Development of this resources has be supported by the following efforts

EarthCube End-User Workshop: Cyberinfrastructure for Paleogeoscience (02/04/2013)
Executive Summary
McNamara Alumni Center
Minneapolis, Minnesota
Collaboration and Cyberinfrastructure for Paleogeoscience (C4P) EarthCube Research Coordination Network (RCN)
Collaboration and Cyberinfrastructure for Paleogeosciences has the goal advance the role of cyberinfrastructure in unraveling large-scale, long-term evolution of the Earth-life system through the study of the geological record. It is a Research Coordination Network funded by the National Science Foundation as part of the EarthCube effort.
http://workspace.earthcube.org/c4p
